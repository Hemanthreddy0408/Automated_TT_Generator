Problem We Solve

College administrators spend days every semester manually creating timetables — battling room conflicts, faculty overlaps, lab constraints, and workload limits using spreadsheets that break with every change. Leave periods make it even worse with no automated recovery.

✦ Key Features
🤖
AI-Powered Schedule Generation
One-click generation of complete conflict-free timetables for all sections, respecting rooms, labs, and break rules simultaneously.
⚖️
Faculty Workload Optimization
Live workload dashboard enforcing per-faculty daily and weekly hour limits. Visual alerts when any faculty is over/under-utilized.
🔄
Live Conflict Resolution
Real-time detection of faculty or room double-bookings with a single "Auto-Resolve" button that reschedules conflicts instantly.
🏖️
Leave-Driven Optimization
When a faculty leave is approved, the system automatically reassigns all affected sessions to free faculty — no manual rescheduling needed.
🔬
Smart Elective Management
Handles shared elective subjects across multiple sections without double-counting faculty hours. Supports multi-section elective slots.
🔔
Real-Time Notifications
Instant in-app alerts to admins and faculty members when timetables change, leave status updates, or new conflicts are detected.
